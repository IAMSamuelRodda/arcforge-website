Questions You're Probably Asking

Do I need to be technical?

[+]

No. You get a login and apps that work like any other software. If you can use Dropbox or Gmail, you can use this. We handle all the technical bits.

What if something breaks?

[+]

The systems are designed to run without babysitting. But if something does go wrong, you've got documentation and we're a message away. We also offer ongoing support packages if you want someone keeping an eye on things.

Can I still use Xero / my accountant / other tools?

[+]

Yes. This handles your files, passwords, and internal systems - not your accounting. Keep using Xero, MYOB, or whatever you're using. This sits alongside it.

What's the catch? Why is this cheaper long-term?

[+]

No catch. SaaS companies charge you monthly forever because that's their business model. We charge once to set it up. You pay hosting costs directly (no markup from us). The software itself is open source - free to use. You're just paying for setup expertise and hosting infrastructure.

What if I want to change something later?

[+]

It's your server. You can hire us for changes, hire someone else, or learn to do it yourself. You're never locked in. We give you all the documentation and access credentials.

Is my data safe?

[+]

Safer than with most cloud services. Your data lives on YOUR server, not shared with thousands of other businesses. All connections are encrypted. And unlike big tech companies, nobody's mining your data for advertising.
