Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need technical knowledge to use this?

[+]

No. Arc Forge handles all technical setup and provides clear documentation. You get a fully working system with user-friendly interfaces (web-based, mobile apps). Your team uses it just like they'd use Google Workspace or any other cloud service.

Can this really replace Google Workspace / Office 365?

[+]

Yes, for file storage, collaboration, and basic productivity needs. Nextcloud provides file sync (like Dropbox), calendar, contacts, and document editing. Plus, you get password management, automation, and VPN that you'd otherwise pay extra for.

What support do you provide after delivery?

[+]

2 weeks of post-launch support is included after project approval. During this period, Arc Forge is available to answer questions, troubleshoot any issues, and ensure smooth operation. After that, optional ongoing monitoring and support packages are available.

What happens if something breaks after the 2-week support period?

[+]

The infrastructure is designed to be stable and low-maintenance. You'll have complete documentation for troubleshooting. Optional ongoing support packages are available that include monitoring, updates, and priority assistance.

Do I need to keep paying you monthly?

[+]

No. After the one-time setup fee and 2-week support period, there are no mandatory ongoing payments. You pay your VPS provider directly (~$75/mo). You own the infrastructure and can manage it yourself or hire anyone you want.
