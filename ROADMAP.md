## Phase 3: Physical Hardware Option (Future)

**Status**: Idea captured - needs deep research after Phase 1 validation

### Concept
Upsell from VPS to physical hardware for businesses wanting TRUE ownership.
- VPS: "Your server in the cloud" (~$75/mo ongoing)
- Hardware: "Your server in your office" (one-time purchase)

### Value Proposition
- Complete ownership - no hosting fees ever
- Data never leaves their premises
- Break-even vs VPS in ~12-24 months
- After that, just electricity + internet

### Research Required
- [ ] Hardware specs equivalent to VPS offering
- [ ] Mini PC vs NUC vs small server options
- [ ] Cost analysis and margin requirements
- [ ] Support model for remote hardware
- [ ] Shipping/installation logistics (Adelaide local vs Australia-wide)
- [ ] Warranty and replacement handling

### Business Rules
- **Critical**: Hardware costs covered in first payment installment
- No inventory risk - order after deposit received

### Thursday Demo Validation Question
> "What if you could have the box sitting in your office instead of paying hosting forever?"

### Napkin Math (Needs Validation)
| Option | Monthly | 3-Year Total | 5-Year Total |
|--------|---------|--------------|--------------|
| VPS | $75 | $2,700 | $4,500 |
| Hardware | $0* | $800-1,500 + setup | $800-1,500 + setup |

*After initial purchase - just electricity/internet

### Go-to-Market
1. Validate VPS demand first (Phase 1)
2. Research and build hardware offering
3. Offer to existing VPS customers as "graduation"
4. Add as premium tier on website

**Detailed notes**: See Joplin note "Future Product: Physical Hardware Server Option" in Arc Forge Business Planning
